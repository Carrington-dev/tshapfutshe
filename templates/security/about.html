The Good News of Salvation

Understanding that salvation is a free gift through Jesus Christ, and baptism is an expression of our faith in His life, death, and resurrection
(Ephesians 2:8-9).

The Importance of the Sabbath

Embracing the Seventh-day Sabbath as a day of rest, worship, and personal connection with God. We will explore the role of the Sabbath in your spiritual growth and commitment to God's commandments.

Living the Adventist Message

Exploring the Three Angels' Messages of Revelation 14, which call us to worship God, live in faith, and share the gospel with the world, especially as we await Christ's soon return.

Health and Wholeness

The Adventist lifestyle emphasizes caring for the body as the temple of God (1 Corinthians 6:19-20). We will discuss how spiritual and physical health go hand in hand in your journey to baptism and beyond.

  • Our Vision for Education in Christ
