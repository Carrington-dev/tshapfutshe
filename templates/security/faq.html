F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pay the first month’s fee on the 1st of January?

Your child’s enrolment will not be secure until we receive the application fee and the first month’s tuition. Enrolment is filled on a first-come, first-served basis.

What can my child have for lunch?

“Why is it important for scholars to eat healthily?”, you might ask.‍ Research has shown that children are able to learn better when they’re well-nourished, and eating healthy meals has been linked to higher marks, improved memory and alertness, and faster information processing. Therefore we encourage our parents to partner with us to provide our scholars with healthy food to fuel their learning.Please see the list at the top of this page for a suggested list of healthy lunches and snacks. The list is not exhaustive, so please check with your scholar’s school if you are unsure of what to pack for daily snack and lunch.

How old must a child be to enrol for Grade R and Grade 1?

At {{ company_title }} Grade R scholars must be turning 5 years old by 30 June of that year to be enrolled.Grade 1 scholars must be turning 6 years old by 30 June of that year to be enrolled.

Where can I buy the {{ company }} uniform?

The {{ company_title }} uniform has been designed to be affordable and versatile. Many of the items can be worn outside of school as well. {{ company_title }} uniforms may be purchased through the following vendors:
1. {{ company_title }} Uniform Shop (located at the school) - open on Wednesdays from 08:00 to 09:00 and Fridays from 13:00 to 14:00. Please contact the school for more information.

Do you have any aftercare or after-school enrichment opportunities?

1. Aftercare is available from dismissal until 5:30 pm five days a week (Monday-Friday) during the school terms.
2. Aftercare is not available during school holidays. The aftercare program is run by {{ company_title }} staff and is available to all scholars enrolled at {{ company_title }}. The aftercare program includes a light meal, homework time, and fun activities. The cost of aftercare is R500 per month.
3. Aftercare is billed separately from tuition and is not included in the monthly fee. Parents are required to sign up for aftercare at the beginning of each term. Please contact the school for more information.

Do you offer a holiday program during term break?

{{ company_title }} does not offer holiday care over term break or public holidays. Please review the instructional calendar to plan care for your child accordingly.

How can I apply to work for {{ company_title }}?

There are many different positions available at {{ company_title }}. To view these positions and to apply please visit our Join the Team page.

Where will {{ company_title }} open schools in future?

Our vision at {{ company_title }} is to open new schools each year throughout South Africa. Please keep following us on our website or Facebook page for updates on where new schools will open each year. Unfortunately, {{ company_title }} is not a franchise, as schools are opened and operated centrally by our Support office.

How do I organise a tour of {{ company_title }}?

If you are a prospective parent interested in viewing our model in action, we invite you to arrange directly with the school closest to you to take a tour during our school hours. You can view our schools’ contact details on our Contact Us page.If you are an educator or external guest interested in a school tour, please e-mail our Marketing department at marketing@cvacademy.co.za.

Does {{ company }} offer extramural activities?

All {{ company_title }} do offer extra-mural activities. These are not included in the fees. Kindly contact your SPARK school directly to enquire about this.

What is the capacity of our class sizes?

{{ company_title }} offers a personalised learning model of education. This model ensures that each scholar receives the academic attention that they require as an individual through one-on-one and small group learning during the school day. In our grades, we have no more than 32 scholars; however, each is receiving the academic attention they require. Read more about our Primary School Model or our High School Model.
